Creativity is for EVERYONE

We are the UK’s leading charity empowering lives, reducing isolation and enhancing wellbeing through the creative arts. We believe that everyone – regardless of circumstances, behaviour, age, gender, race or disability – should have access to the power of creativity.

Our professional artists run creative arts projects with people facing challenges in their lives. They are given a chance to express themselves, build skills, meet other people and nurture their wellbeing through animation, dance, drama, music, painting, photography and a host of other artforms.
