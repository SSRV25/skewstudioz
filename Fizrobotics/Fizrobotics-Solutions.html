What is the right age of learning ?

Learning is a process which consists of dedication, practice and the right time to begin. One should know what is the right time to start a journey of Learning. Our Ancient Saints know the best of it.

In the Ancient Times, why were kids sent to the Gurukul (Schools) at an early age?

Only at the age of 7 years they have been sent there and once their education has been completed at the age of 25 years they are supposed to live there. Because, according to our Ancient Saints, this is the best age for students which helps in personality development and increases their confidence, sense of discipline, intellect and mindfulness which is necessary even today to face the world that lay ahead.

When we start learning at an early age, it becomes easier to make complicated things clear in a simpler way. There is always the right time to Explore, Learn, Make and Innovate.

How your KIDS will learn ?

Activity Based Learning

In Activity Based learning, students are meant to be part of the class as an active participant. They are not only the listeners of the class but to avoid the boredom for students we have indulged them in various activities which gives them Hands-On-Training Experience of Live Projects.

Project Based Learning

Project Based Learning curriculum is designed in such a way to indulge students in real life problems. Project Based Learning helps students to develop problem solving and solution building skills. Students are assigned with different projects and they have to come with the best idea.

Activity ​Experience Based Learning 

In Experience Based Learning students use their prior concrete experience to understand the interesting concepts. Students experiencing the solutions for their existing experiences. Experience Based Learning helps students to understand the abstract conceptualization with active experimentation.
